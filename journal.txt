## 복원 절차
1. 백업 폴더 → ~/sketch_robot_ws 로 복사
2. cd ~/sketch_robot_ws
3. colcon build --packages-select sketch_control --symlink-install
4. source install/setup.bash
5. 사용:
   - Phase 1 (Python GUI): ros2 launch sketch_control phase1_python.launch.py
   - Phase 2 (Unity GUI):  ros2 launch sketch_control phase2_unity.launch.py
